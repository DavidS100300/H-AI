3. Project Description

3.1. Topic

The purpose of the project is to innovate a recruitment application utilizing the adaptation of Artificial Intelligence into traditional human resource practices, particularly for the hiring process. Specifically, the application will comprise a wide range of key core human resource features using artificial intelligence such as CV scanning, rating, skills assessment, etc. By automating these traditional activities, we would thrive to eliminate these time-consuming and manual tasks that were done using spreadsheets or simple structure, enhancing working process and bring about utmost efficiency. The primary objective is to bring about a cohesive and efficient working process for recruiters and candidates alike, streamlining their navigation through the recruitment process with ease.

3.2. Motivation

The motivation behind our project is to utilise AI technology within a business context to transform the recruitment process. The ultimate goal of our project is to improve hiring outcomes, reducing noteworthy working time and cost savings implementing task automation. The statement is in accordance with contemporary information technology patterns as it highlights our capacity to implement nascent technologies in a pragmatic manner, demonstrating knowledge in software engineering, data analytics, and user interface/user experience. Upon engaging in the project, we would be able to develop our proficiency in information technology, specifically artificial intelligence, as well as further soft skills in critical thinking, etc thereby presenting a persuasive argument to current business context regarding the utilisation of technology for consequential resolutions.

3.3. Landscape

Within the realm of recruitment systems and products, there exist numerous functioning solutions and competitors that incorporate artificial intelligence technology. Popular services include Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) such as Workday, Greenhouse, and Jobvite, which streamline recruitment processes. In addition, automated screening tools that utilize artificial intelligence, such as HireVue and Ideal, have been developed to speed up the initial assessment of resumes. AI-powered talent sourcing tools such as Entelo and HiringSolved are utilized for candidate identification, while technical capabilities are assessed through skills evaluation platforms like Codility and HackerRank. Our project stands out by combining these key features into a comprehensive AI-powered recruitment application that integrates automated resume screening, intelligent candidate sourcing, bias reduction measures, and data-driven decision-making. Our application's strength lies in its ability to provide holistic and end-to-end recruitment support, offering a unified platform that brings together multiple AI-driven features for enhanced efficiency, accuracy, and an improved candidate and recruiter experience.

4.3. Scopes and Limits

Scopes

This project focuses on implementing AI technology to revolutionize the recruitment process in businesses. In our first draft (first idea), we planned to integrate AI systems in various stages of the recruitment process including screening, analyzing performance, interview, etc. Machine learning and deep learning will be necessary in the development phase because they can help us to extract higher-level features of data and we all have enough knowledge to use and develop them. However, due to time and technology limitations, we can not use AI to interview candidates because they can not generate emotions and empathize with human feelings and it will be against ethics. Despite the fact that it can analyze resumes and application forms, it can not analyze personality, motivation of a person. Therefore, we decided to use this AI system as a tool and businesses should rely on human’s decisions in the recruitment process. We also trained our system into different sets of ethical and moral rules in order to possibly avoid bias and provide the appropriate analysis. As a result, this system will have the including features:

  • Scan resume of candidates
  • Analyze past performance/stats and give a full report to businesses
  • Provide rating of a candidates
  • Automate send email to candidate
  • Automate schedule interview for candidates and recruiters

Limitations

There are certain limits that AI engineers and businesses need to consider despite this is the project using AI to enhance the recruitment process. Firstly, the insights and recommendations that this system provides will heavily depend on the quality and the relevance of data that AI engineers trained it on. Secondly, because each recruiter will have a different mindset and criteria to select the appropriate candidates, it will be difficult for AI engineers to observe and analyze this enormous and diverse data. Thirdly, AI is not human, it can not have enough intelligence to create different kinds of emotions due to the limitations of technology. Therefore, a business can not replace the needs of humans by using this AI system to interview people. Lastly, this system will only assist the Human Resource Management department in a business in screening and evaluating candidates based on their past performance and stats. Furthermore, it will not account for different major factors in a working environment that significantly affect the suitability and the effectiveness of an employee in a specific role or department. As a result, it can not define how well they adapt into the business working environment and how effectively they communicate with others colleagues. Therefore, this system is only a tool that supports the HR department to fasten the process of recruitment rather than a replacement system for human decision-making which are stakeholders and shareholders.

4.8. Risks

As you know, AI is a very powerful system that can bring numerous benefits to businesses and HR departments. There are still some potential risks and ethical issues related to this tool. By proactively approaching these risks and implementing appropriate solutions, businesses can enhance the system while minimizing potential drawbacks.Here are some key risks to consider:

Firstly, new employees may not perform as effectively as their previous jobs. The reason for this is because real performance is based on the adaptation of employees on different sets of backgrounds and environments while the old historical data and resumes that AI learn only show the performance of candidates on the previous job. Additionally, each person possesses unique personalities and characteristics that can influence their performance in a new role.

Secondly, security issues and privacy issues are also big challenges for businesses. This is because our AI system requires different kinds of data including resumes, application forms, and even some sensitive personal data such as behaviors, personality characteristics, biometric information (race, face recognition fingerprint, etc). As a result, this will raise some data security concerns for users (candidates and recruiters) and businesses.

Thirdly, despite the fact that the use of our AI system is to avoid bias in recruiters, recruitment processes using AI may also encounter some bias. The reason for this is AI systems need to train from a wide range of statistics and actual data and these may be biased or reflect existing societal prejudices.

Lasly, by utilizing almost everything using the AI system, it may cause a negative impact on a candidate's experience by reducing human interaction. Many people with less skills and abilities than the rest will be frustrated because their jobs completely depend on AI and this may cause a negative impact on society by creating more unemployment because companies will prefer better employees. This will raise enormous ethical problems.
